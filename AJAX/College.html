College

The Master's College

I was a junior in high school when our guidance counselor told us of a Christian college in America that they were collaborating with. It seemed interesting to me but I didn't really consider it until during the summer before my senior year, the Philippines GO team of TMC came and helped us in our community service. It was there where I got to meet Dr. Lisa LaGeorge and she and my guidance counselor encouraged me to try and apply. After that, the rest was history!

Life @ TMC

Currently, I am a freshman international student in The Master's College! I live in HOTCHKISS Upper Front and am having an awesome time. For the past few months, I have seen myself grow in different aspects: spritually, emotionally and mentally. I've also been able to develop many friendships here and am looking forward to the next three years of my college life.
